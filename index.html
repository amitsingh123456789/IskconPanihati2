Who are We?

Panihati Vaishnav Seva Dham is a collaboration of Panihati Unnayan Trust.Women Water Warriors, Arrival Ganga Mission and Sri Krishna Education and Cultural Trust,working to fulfil the ambitious objectives of unfolding the glorious history of Gaudiya Vaishnavism in Panihati and promoting sustainable human development or living in harmony with nature especially with Ganga which forms a principal aspect of Vaishnava culture and tradition.

OUR PRIME ACTIVITIES

  • Cleaning, renovation and beautification of Ganga ghat.

  • Beautification of Dandamahotsavtala and Gauranga ghat, two crucial spots in Panihati, associated with countless pastimes of Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityananda, so as to ultimately present Gaudiya Vaishnavism as a means to re-establish the lost connection between nature and religion.

  • To conduct workshops, trainings and conferences to revive Vaishnava culture in Panihati.

  • To promote religious tourism in Panihati

  • To work for richness and enhancement of biodiversity and health of environment

  • To conduct water literacy and river rejuvenation awareness programmes

  • To coordinate with authorities for mitigation measures

  • To mobilise schools, colleges and other stakeholders in river rejuvenation activities
